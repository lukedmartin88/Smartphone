Between [Parent/Guardian Name] and [Child's Name]

Purpose

The purpose of this agreement is to ensure that [Child's Name] understands the responsibilities and expectations that come with owning and using a smartphone. This contract is designed to encourage responsible, safe, and respectful behaviour online and offline.

Parent Commitments

We, [Parent/Guardian Name], will:

  • ☑Provide [Child's Name] with a smartphone.
  • ☑Pay for the monthly service (unless otherwise agreed).
  • ☑Support and guide [Child's Name] in using the phone responsibly.
  • ☑Respect [Child's Name]'s reasonable privacy while ensuring their safety.

Responsibilities

I, [Child's Name], agree to the following:

1. Respect & Responsibility

  • I understand that the phone belongs to my parents and is being loaned to me.
  • I will treat the phone with care. If it is lost or broken, I understand I may be responsible for repair or replacement costs.
  • I will not use the phone to lie, bully, harass, or hurt others in any way.

2. Safety

  • I will not talk to, text, or share personal information with strangers.
  • I will never share my passwords, address, school name, or other private information online.
  • I will tell my parents immediately if I encounter anything upsetting or dangerous online. I know I will not get in trouble for bringing these things to their attention.

3. Screen Time & Use

  • The phone will never be taken to my room without permission, and it will not stay in my room overnight.
  • When the phone is in "downtime" mode or when it is bedtime, it will be placed in its designated charging holder in the porch.
  • I will not use the phone during family meals or family activities unless I am given permission.
  • I will balance phone use with homework, outdoor activities, and real-world friendships.

4. Apps & Downloads

  • I will ask for permission before downloading any new apps or games.
  • I will not access inappropriate websites or content.
  • I will not try to bypass parental controls or restrictions.

5. Social Media

  • I understand that the legal age for most social media and messaging apps is 13 Years Old. I will not create an account or download any such app without a specific discussion and agreement with my parents.

6. Camera & Photos

  • I will always ask for permission before taking a photo or video of anyone.
  • I will not take or share photos or videos that could be embarrassing, hurtful, or inappropriate for others or myself.
  • I will be mindful of others' privacy and not take photos or videos in private places (like bathrooms or changing rooms).

7. Financial Responsibility

  • I will not make any in-app purchases, online purchases, or subscriptions of any kind unless I have clear permission from my parents first.
  • I understand that using too much mobile data may result in extra charges, and I will manage my data usage responsibly.

8. Additional Rules

  • I will not attempt to remove or bypass any of the parental restrictions set on the phone.
  • I will hand the device to my parents to inspect at any time, upon request and without protest. If I do not, I understand that the phone will be taken from me.

Consequences

I understand that if I break this agreement, I may lose phone privileges for a period of time. If the misuse is serious or happens repeatedly, this privilege may be revoked permanently.

Acknowledgement

By signing below, we agree to this contract and promise to review it together regularly to make sure it is still working for our family.

[Parent/Guardian Name]

[Child's Name]
